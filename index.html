New to Beekeeping?

Getting Started Guide

🐝

Choose Your Bees

Italian bees are gentle and great for beginners. Carniolans are cold-hardy for northern climates.

🏠

Set Up Your Hive

Morning sun, evening shade, windbreak, and a close water source is the ideal setup.

🛡️

Protect Yourself

Full suit or jacket with veil, gloves, and a smoker. Never skip the veil!

🍯

Harvest Honey

Expect 20–60 lbs of honey per hive by year two.
